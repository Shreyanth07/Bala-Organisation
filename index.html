About us and our partnered Members

Bala Organisation was started in the motive of providing all the good and useful needs to people who cannot afford and who are in need for helping hands. We initially wanted to help people with basic needs then later we found out that education, food and medicine are the primary needs of the people and we focussed on providing the best for it. Hence to overcome this issue of people we created this website to overcome the donation and to provide the students with the education for the affordable money they expect with high standards education system and advanced technology in the schools for bright future of the students.

Our partnered team have also joined along with us in this campaign to provide the best services at very low marginal cost from their end.

EDUTRAIN

EDUTRAIN is one of the leading and developing competitive exam training centre with career guidance that provides training and tution services at free of cost. They have well experienced faculties from the top schools and colleges in India.

EDUTRAIN

RIDS

Ranga Institute of Dental Sciences provides graduate degree in BDS. RIDS provides low cost education to people and the students who are graduated through Bala Organisation with good grades will be awarded scholarship admission.

RIDS

BALA DIAGNOSTICS

BALA Diagnostics is the subsidary of Bala Organisations which provides diagnosis service as a medical domain to provide a free of cost to people who are below poverty. It has expert medical team with high standard equipments.

BALA DIAG

RENGA DENTAL

RENGA Dental is the subsidary of RIDS which is a dental hospital that provides a free of cost medical service to the people who are below poverty. It has expert doctors and high standard equipments

RENGA

HOOKUP MEDS

HOOKUP Meds are the online pharma guiders which guides the medical store that contains the medicines required for the patients and delivers the medicine to the patients with ease of service.

HOOKUPMEDS

VEETU SORU

VEETU Soru are the leading food service company delivering food to the people at low cost and also donates the leftover food to the poor people. They accept any money given by the people.

VEETU SORU

Our Team

When Bala Org was started; it was a team of people who came together from different fields and walks of life to make a discernable change to society & Education with the work that they would do using education as the primary tool.

Currently, the Bala Org team comprises of people from different walks of life who have all come together with their varied experiences to work towards this one mission. The team includes people from diverse backgrounds including social workers, activists, PhDs, MBAs, CAs, civil servants, bankers, corporate professionals, consultants, academicians, medical professionals, etc.
